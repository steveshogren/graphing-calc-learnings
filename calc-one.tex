\section{Factoring}

Use the quadratic formula:
$$(x+a)(x+b)$$
$$x^2+xb+ax+ab$$
$$x^2+(a+b)x+ab$$

To factor $$x^2+10x+9$$
$$a+b = 10$$
$$ab = 9$$
Factors of 9 are 1,3,9
$$1+9 = 10$$
$$1*9 = 9$$
So
$$(x+1)(x+9)$$

To factor $$x^2-1$$

Use Sum of cubes:
$$a^3+b^3$$
$$(a+b)(a^2-ab+b^2)$$
Use Difference of cubes:
$$a^3-b^3$$
$$(a-b)(a^2+ab+b^2)$$

To solve a division:

$$\frac{x^3-8}{x-2}$$

Power Rule:
